The joy of Enviro-Week!

In this edition, we provide a vivid review of Enviro-Week. Picture this: The rhythmic sound of paddles cutting through the water as students kayaked under the clear blue sky, the air filled with excitement and camaraderie. For the eighth-grade students, the ropes course offered an unforgettable challenge. Imagine the thrill of their voices as they encouraged one another from the treetops, a symphony of laughter and determination. This immersive experience brought students closer to nature and to each other. Stay tuned for an upcoming interview that will offer further insights into the rope course adventure.
