The joy of Enviro-Week!

In this edition, we provide a vivid review of Enviro-Week. Picture this: the rhythmic sound of paddles cutting through the water as students kayak under a clear blue sky, the air filled with excitement and camaraderie. For the eighth-grade students, the ropes course offered an unforgettable challenge. Imagine the thrill of their voices as they encouraged one another from the treetops, a symphony of laughter and determination. This immersive experience brought students closer to nature and to each other. Stay tuned for an upcoming interview that will offer further insights into the rope course adventure. SPORTS

Hey sports fans! Here’s your weekly update on what’s happening in the big leagues:

NHL (Hockey) The NHL returns from its 19-day Olympic pause in Milan, igniting a chaotic sprint to the 2026 playoffs. The Tampa Bay Lightning, leading the East, look to stars like Nikita Kucherov to maintain their elite form. However, the league-leading Colorado Avalanche is challenging for the top spot, battling the fatigue typical of modern champions. In a massive surprise, the Detroit Red Wings are officially in playoff contention, finally snapping their long rebuilding cycle. At the same time, the New York Rangers struggle near the bottom of their division. The New York Rangers are also fighting with the Washington Capitals for a playoff spot. For the Washington Capitals to make the playoffs, they need a nearly perfect win streak out of their 10 games remaining.

NBA (Baskoobol) The NBA enters its final regular-season sprint following the 2026 All-Star break at the new Intuit Dome in Los Angeles, where Anthony Edwards solidified his superstar status with a dominant MVP performance. In a massive surprise, the Detroit Pistons have officially snapped their rebuilding cycle, clinching the top seed in the East over the Boston Celtics. However, the Minnesota Timberwolves and Denver Nuggets are challenging for Western supremacy, aiming to overcome the fatigue of a grueling 82-game schedule. While the Phoenix Suns struggle with depth and injuries to key personnel, the Los Angeles Lakers are making a late-season push to avoid the Play-In tournament.
