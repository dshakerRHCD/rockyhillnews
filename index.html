The joy of Enviro-Week!

In this edition, we provide a vivid review of Enviro-Week. Picture this: the rhythmic sound of paddles cutting through the water as students kayak under a clear blue sky, the air filled with excitement and camaraderie. For the eighth-grade students, the ropes course offered an unforgettable challenge. Imagine the thrill of their voices as they encouraged one another from the treetops, a symphony of laughter and determination.
